Ukulima Smart Policies

We at Ukulima Smart care about protecting both our farmers and customers by ensuring fair and transparent transactions.

Farmer Policies

Accurate Information Policy

  • Farmers must provide accurate and truthful information about their produce, including quality, quantity, and price. Misleading descriptions or false information will result in penalties or account suspension.

    • Quality Assurance Policy

  • All listed produce must meet the quality standards promised in the description. Substandard or damaged produce must not be listed or delivered.

    • Timely Delivery Policy

  • Farmers must ensure that the produce is ready within the agreed timeframe. Delays should be communicated to the customer promptly, and reasonable efforts must be made to resolve any issues.

    • Refund and Return Policy

  • Farmers must have a clear policy for refunds and returns in case the produce does not meet the agreed standards. This should be communicated to customers before the transaction is completed.

    • Prohibited Items Policy

  • Certain items may be prohibited from sale due to legal or safety reasons. Farmers must adhere to these restrictions and ensure that all listed produce complies with local regulations.

    • Pricing Policy

  • Farmers should set prices that reflect the current market value and should not exploit customers with excessively high prices. Conversely, undercutting the market value significantly can also be restricted to maintain fair competition.

    • Customer Policies

    Authentic Transactions Policy

  • Customers must use their real identity and provide accurate contact information. Fraudulent activity, such as using fake identities or stolen payment information, will result in account suspension and possible legal action.

    • Payment Policy

  • Customers must ensure timely payment for the produce they purchase. Failure to complete payments or attempting chargebacks without valid reasons will lead to penalties.

    • Product Inspection Policy

  • Customers are encouraged to inspect the produce upon delivery and report any discrepancies immediately. Claims made after after certain period may not be entertained.

    • Communication Policy

  • Customers should communicate respectfully and promptly with farmers. Harassment or abusive behavior will not be tolerated and may result in account suspension.

    • Return and Refund Policy

  • Customers should follow the farmer's return and refund policies. Unreasonable demands for refunds or returns without valid reasons may not be honored.

    • Transport Service Provider Policies

    Accurate Information Policy

  • Transport service providers must provide accurate and truthful information about their services, including pricing, and availability. Misleading descriptions or false information will result in penalties or account suspension.

    • Timely Service Policy

  • Transport providers must ensure timely delivery of services as agreed upon with the customer. Delays should be communicated promptly, and reasonable efforts must be made to resolve any issues.

    • Customer Service Policy

  • Transport providers should communicate respectfully and promptly with customers. Harassment or abusive behavior will not be tolerated and may result in account suspension.

    • Pricing Transparency Policy

  • Transport providers should offer transparent pricing without hidden fees. All charges should be clearly communicated to the customer before the service is provided.

    • Insurance Policy

  • Transport providers must have adequate insurance coverage to protect against any potential damage or loss of goods during transit. Proof of insurance may be required.

    • Dispute Resolution Policy

  • In case of disputes, transport providers should make reasonable efforts to resolve issues amicably. Any unresolved disputes can be reported to Ukulima Smart for mediation.

    • Fraudulent Incident Reporting!!!

  • If you encounter any fraudulent incidents or suspicious activities, please report them immediately to our support team at +254-700-123-456. Your vigilance helps us maintain a safe and trustworthy community for everyone.
